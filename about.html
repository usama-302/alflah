Our Company

Butt Group of Industries; Butt Silk Mills (Pvt.) Limited, Butt Carpet Industries (Pvt.) Limited, Butt Spinning Industries and Panoramic Hotel are family-owned companies located in different cities in the province of Punjab, Pakistan.

We are the pioneer in pile fabric and our third generation is involved in manufacturing of finest quality velvet fabric, jacquard velvet fabrics and prayer rugs/mats in Pakistan.

Mission

Butt Silk Mills prioritizes customer satisfaction both domestically and internationally. With regular evaluations of our quality policy, we work together as a team to ensure we are moving forward.

Our quality management allows us to offer a quality product at competitive prices. We aim to improve our market share while being aware of changes in environment conditions and the needs of consumers while also investing time and capital into research and development of technology and human resources. We aim to use our quality products to make us the market leader in the international market. Our monthly capacity of approximately 350,000 meters with a range that includes satin, velvet and knitted upholstery as well as velvet velour carpets offers our customers a top quality product.

From this platform of trust, and using our resources efficiently and effectively, we will finish the best production for you the first time. Our team spirit and efforts to continuously improve will help maintain a high level of customer satisfaction. Our History

In early 1950s, Mr Muhammad Aslam Butt started off as a small manufacturer of flat woven fabric on hand looms. A few years later this manufacturing process was switched on power looms then pile fabric (velvet) in 1960. In order to be the leader and to produce the best quality velvet fabric, it was realized that innovative professional work force, technology, and raw material (yarn) must go hand in hand. So Asia Spinning Mills (acrylic yarn spinning) and Kashmir Woolen Mills (carpet yarn spinning) came into existence in 1973 and 1975 respectively. Best available professionals and skilled workforce were hired to stay ahead of the competition. As the time progressed, we acquired the best technology available in our field. It is an admitted fact that we have the most modern equipment (machinery) available in Pakistan at present. We try to stay at least 15 years ahead of our competition and let them follow us.

In conclusion, ever since its inception in 1960 we have directed all of our efforts and resources in developing the best quality products. It is our constant endeavor to raise standards in designing and in quality. While creating new designs, our designers keep in mind the varied moods, colors and aesthetic look which will have soothing effect on us. Gujranwala Business Centre (GBC) is a public sector company registered under section 42 of the Companies Act 2017, initiated in 2010 as a PSDP project. It works under the Ministry of Industries & Production, Government of Pakistan, to promote and develop Gujranwala's industrial sector, the 3rd largest in the country, at both national and international levels. It is a dynamic and influential organization that fosters economic growth, empowers businesses, and shapes the local business landscape, which consists of more than 21,000 SMEs & Cottage industries. International Diplomats, Trade Bodies, Joint Chambers of Commerce, and Trade Delegations regularly engage with GBC for B2B connectivity, enhancing international trade and economic cooperation within the business community of Gujranwala.

At the forefront of GBC's notable initiatives stands the Made In Gujranwala (MIG) Enclosure, a flagship project designed to showcase the best industrial products of Gujranwala to the world under one roof. The MIG Enclosure features a comprehensive representation of all major business sectors of Gujranwala, including Ceramics, Sanitaryware, Cookware, Sanitary Fittings, Textiles, Motors & Pumps, Home Appliances, Rice, and more. It provides an excellent opportunity to showcase the city's industrial prowess, facilitate networking, and attract investment. Similarly, GBC also facilitates the formation of trade delegations from Gujranwala to explore new markets and avail the opportunities for result-oriented growth, partnership, and strategic alliances with global partners from different countries. It offers strategic business solutions and information for improving trade, economic cooperation, strengthening the trade base, and improving exports for the business community of Gujranwala.
